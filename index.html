How it works
3 steps. That's it.
Scan, type, copy. ClipBySu bridges your devices in seconds.
01
📱

Scan QR on Phone

Open the QR tab on your PC and scan the code with your phone camera — instantly opens ClipBySu. No URL typing needed.

02
✍️

Type or Paste Text

Type anything on your phone — passwords, links, long notes, addresses. Tap Send. Done in 2 seconds.

03
💻

Copy on Your PC

Switch to your PC. Your text is already waiting. Click Copy and paste it anywhere you need.

Go Live
Step-by-step: Make it a real website
Follow these steps to deploy ClipBySu on the internet for free — forever.
1
FREE

📥 Download This File

Click the download button above this page to save clipbysu.html to your computer.

Rename the file to exactly: index.html — this is critical for GitHub Pages to work.

Rename: clipbysu.html → index.html
2
FREE

🐙 Create a GitHub Account

Go to github.com → Click Sign up → Enter your email, create a password, choose a username.

Verify your email address and complete the setup wizard. GitHub is free forever.

https://github.com/signup
3
FREE

📁 Create a New Repository

After logging in → Click the "+" icon (top right) → Select "New repository"

Name it: clipbysu · Set to Public · Check ✅ "Add a README file" · Click Create repository

Repository name: clipbysu
4
FREE

⬆️ Upload index.html

Inside your new repository → Click "Add file" → Click "Upload files"

Drag and drop your index.html file into the upload box → Write commit message: "Add ClipBySu website" → Click Commit changes

5
FREE

🌐 Enable GitHub Pages

In your repository → Click Settings (top menu) → Scroll to "Pages" in the left sidebar

Under Branch → Select main → Folder: / (root) → Click Save → Wait 1–2 minutes ⏳

6
FREE

✅ Your Site is Live!

Your ClipBySu website is now live at this URL (replace YOUR-USERNAME):

https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/clipbysu

Share this link with yourself — open on PC + phone at the same time and start syncing!

7
OPTIONAL

📱 Get a Custom Domain

Want www.clipbysu.com instead of the GitHub URL? Buy a domain (~₹800/year) from Namecheap or GoDaddy.

In GitHub Pages Settings → Enter your domain under "Custom domain" → Follow the DNS instructions. Takes ~24 hours to activate.

Recommended: namecheap.com · porkbun.com
8
OPTIONAL

📲 Use QR to Open on Phone

Once your site is live → Open it on your PC → Go to the QR Code tab in the app

The QR will auto-fill your website URL → Scan it with your phone camera → Both devices open ClipBySu → Start syncing instantly!

9
PRO TIP

🔖 Bookmark on Both Devices

On your PC: Press Ctrl+D to bookmark ClipBySu in your browser.

On your phone: Open the site in Safari/Chrome → Tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen" — it will look and feel like a real app on your phone!
